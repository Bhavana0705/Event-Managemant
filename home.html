What services do you offer as a wedding planne?

We offer a wide range of services, including venue selection, vendor management, budget planning, decor and styling, guest management, wedding invitations, entertainment, photography and videography, catering, transportation, and honeymoon planning.

How far in advance should I book a wedding planner?

It is advisable to book a wedding planner at least 6-12 months in advance to ensure availability and sufficient time for planning and execution.

What is the cost of hiring a wedding planner?

The cost of hiring a wedding planner in Bangalore varies depending on the services required, the scale of the wedding, and the planner's experience. Generally, prices can range from INR 1.5 lakhs to INR 10 lakhs or more.

Do you offer customizable wedding packages?

Yes, we offer customizable wedding packages to suit your preferences, requirements, and budget. You can choose the services you need, and we'll create a personalized package for you.

Can you work with my preferred vendors?

We are happy to collaborate with your preferred vendors, provided they meet our quality standards and are available on your wedding dates. We can also suggest additional vendors from our trusted network, if needed.

What are the things to consider in wedding decoration?

When considering wedding decoration, it's important to factor in the venue size, theme, color palette, budget, cultural or religious traditions, and the couple's personal preferences.
